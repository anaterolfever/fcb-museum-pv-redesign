About the experience

The Barça Museum is where fans relive the club’s greatest moments: Champions League and league titles, the story of FC Barcelona across decades, and spaces such as the Messi Zone with individual awards. The 360° macro room and Barça Virtual Dream take the emotion further — and RoboKeeper lets you shoot against the world’s fastest robotic keeper (included where your ticket package includes it).

Finish with the construction viewpoint to follow the transformation of Spotify Camp Nou. Choose Basic Ticket, Total Experience, Virtual Experience, or Flexible Basic Ticket and secure your visit.
